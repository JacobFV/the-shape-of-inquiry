# Motion language

- Cuts every 6–10 seconds.
- Text appears in short pulses; no paragraph held for dozens of seconds.
- Alternate real substrate, abstract inference, metric burst, and reflective stillness.
- Use real lab photo crops, ROI labels, and procedural overlays rather than fake sci-fi UI.
- Equations slide in like instrument readouts, then leave.
- Graphs and event streams build over time, not as static diagrams.

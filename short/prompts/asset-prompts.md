## Additional shot prompts

### Human precondition / calibration labor
Close documentary shot of gloved hands placing a color calibration card beside disposable pH strips on a matte black lab bench; shallow depth of field; tungsten lab light; no faces; no branding.

### Pipette actuation macro
Extreme macro of a pipette tip releasing one droplet into a transparent well plate; fluid meniscus visible; realistic optics; no sci-fi overlays.

### Modular end-effector exchange
Close shot of a small robotic arm toolhead docking with a 3D-printed interchangeable end-effector; screws, magnets, cable strain relief visible; practical lab bench realism.

### Multi-camera sensing
Two compact machine-vision cameras aimed at a tabletop chemistry workspace; calibration checkerboard or color card visible; black rails, cables, modest research prototype.

### Abstract inference
Minimal black-background technical animation: event stream lanes, causal graph edges, posterior distribution narrowing, sparse mathematical typography; no neon hologram aesthetic.

### pH strip segmentation
Macro shot of pH strips with color patches, overlaid with small rectangular segmentation boxes and calibrated color sample points; precise, paper-quality.


# Audio direction

No generated audio files are included. Use this as the production direction.

## Narration
Record in a low, calm, impersonal voice. Avoid trailer cadence. Deliver at roughly 165–185 words per minute.

## Sound design search terms
- quiet laboratory room tone
- soft servo motor movement close mic
- micropipette liquid drop close sound
- relay click laboratory equipment
- camera shutter machine vision
- low subharmonic drone no melody
- sparse processed cello laboratory documentary

## Suno / music prompt
Sparse, mathematical, tectonic research-film score. Low sustained contrabass or processed cello fundamentals, quiet subharmonic pulses, occasional muted piano or vibraphone single notes, granular room texture, no melody in the commercial sense, no heroic rise, no trailer drums, no glossy synthwave, no emotional swell. Tempo mostly undefined. Feels like a laboratory at night and a theorem slowly resolving. Leave room for narration and silence.

## Negative prompt
No EDM, no corporate optimism bed, no trailer riser, no heroic strings, no sentimental piano, no sci-fi glitch spam, no Marvel trailer hit.
